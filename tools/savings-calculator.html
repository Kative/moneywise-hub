How Much Emergency Fund Do I Need?

Most financial experts recommend saving 3 to 6 months of essential expenses. Here's a quick guide:

  • 3 months — If you have a stable job, dual income household, or low fixed expenses
  • 6 months — The gold standard for most people. Covers job loss, medical emergencies, car repairs
  • 9-12 months — Recommended if you're self-employed, freelance, have variable income, or are the sole earner

Where to Keep Your Emergency Fund

Your emergency fund should be in a high-yield savings account (HYSA) — accessible when you need it, but earning interest while it sits there. Avoid investing your emergency fund in stocks — you need it liquid and safe.
