Snowball vs. Avalanche: Which Is Better?

Avalanche saves more money on interest. Snowball builds more motivation with quick wins. Mathematically, avalanche always wins. Psychologically, snowball often works better because people stay motivated.

Our recommendation: Pick the one you'll actually stick with. A slightly less-optimal strategy you follow is infinitely better than a perfect strategy you abandon.
