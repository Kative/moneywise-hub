Debt Snowball vs. Avalanche: Which Method Gets You Debt-Free Faster?

📖 9 min read📅 March 2026

Both methods work. The difference is in how they work — and which matches your personality.

The Debt Snowball Method

Strategy: Pay minimums on all debts. Throw extra at the smallest balance first. Quick wins build motivation.

  • ✅ Quick wins keep you motivated
  • ✅ Simplifies bills faster
  • ❌ May pay more total interest

The Debt Avalanche Method

Strategy: Pay minimums on all debts. Throw extra at the highest interest rate first. Saves the most money.

  • ✅ Saves the most on interest
  • ✅ Mathematically optimal
  • ❌ First payoff might take months

Which Should YOU Choose?

Choose Snowball if you need motivation and quick wins. Choose Avalanche if you're disciplined and want to save the most money.

The best debt payoff method is the one you'll actually stick with.

The Hybrid Approach

Pay off your smallest debt first (quick win), then switch to avalanche for the rest. Best of both worlds.
